Learning Plan

  1. What is your long term goal or career pathway?

    To become a successful software engineer and build stuff on my own.

  2. A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

    Strengths - I’m hardworking, never give up on what I started. If I don’t know something I don't hesitate to seek help/guidance.

    Limitations - Coding is a new language which i’m learning, so, it’s taking time for me to understand concepts and implement those concepts in practice.

  3. What skills (non-technical/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

    Public speaking - talking confidently in front of people is what I really like to work on.

  4. A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme. This should include:
    An explanation of how you will manage yourself to work productively and safely with other learners, Facilitators and industry/community representatives

    I decided to leave my job to fully concentrate on this learning opportunity. I'll do my best to minimize distractions and ensure productive learning. I'm committed to effective communication with my peers and facilitators, aiming for a seamless and collaborative environment. I'll actively listen to others and communicate clearly and respectfully. If conflicts arise, I'll strive to resolve them peacefully through teamwork and compromise.

    What you will do when things are building up and starting to get to you

    I'll do my best to avoid feeling overwhelmed by pressure. While I know it can be challenging at times, I'll take breaks to manage stress. I'll also prioritize the most important tasks and focus on completing them. I'll seek support from family and friends when i need it. Additionally, I'll break down larger tasks into smaller, manageable ones and set realistic goals. Finally, I'll avoid committing to additional responsibilities if I'm already feeling overwhelmed.

  5. A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

    I will seek help from facilitators and peers through discord or in person during boot camp and external help through other websites such as google, stack overflow.

  6. A description of what you expect from the Facilitation team

    As I'm a beginner in coding, I might ask silly questions, so, please be patient and don’t get annoyed with me. Thanks :)

  7. Any scheduling information such as block-out times when you are committed to other things. For example, you might have whānau commitments like school drop-offs. It's important to plan these in.

    I’m completely committed to my learning during these four months so there are no block-out times. My family is ensuring that the only thing I do is keep learning.
